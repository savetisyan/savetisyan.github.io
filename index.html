About me

In 2016, I started my PhD under the supervision of Prof. Shravan Vasishth and Dr. Sol Lago. My research focuses on memory mechanisms that underlie the computation of grammatical relations between sentence constituents. I am currently using behavioral measures (e.g., self-paced reading, eye-tracking) and Bayesian modelling to investigate how number agreement is computed between subject and verb in Eastern Armenian, with a focus on why this computation can fail in the presence of other linguistic items with similar features in working memory.
